There are simple rules. Post on the correct cities message board and only post sightings on the city message boards. Do not clutter the city message boards with conversation.

Any messages containing bigotry, doxing, or irrelevant content will be deleted.

Why?

Social Media requires accounts. Some people dont have access to accounts or do not wish to have this ype of acctivity linked to their accounts. This site gives people a place to easily check if their errands are safe to run or report a sighting without concern for access or judgment.
