Development Plan

This project is bare bones, and if the community uses it, there will need to be improvements!

Oversight

The goal of this site is to help keep the community safe! Oversight is very important. This is something that will come with time and matters heavily to Llama (the admin).

Content Moderation

Content moderation is very important! This is how we delete content that distracts from or conflicts with this website’s goals. We have a simple moderation method in place for now, but its effectiveness will need to be improved over time.

Validating Information

Posting I.C.E. sightings is a good start! Unfortunately, it's easy for resources like this to lose control and lose their effectiveness. Validating sightings is very important! Right now, we are in the community development phase, but eventually we will need to make sure that we are posting true information. We do not want to scare our neighbors. As we develop this community, we will work on validating claims and making our efforts more robust and effective.

Translation

As this project builds, we need to be considerate of the communities we are trying to protect. Making sure the information is accessible and properly translated is very important.

Resources

We are not the only resource, and we are far from the most effective resource. We need to make a resources page that points people to more robust support networks and information.

Thank you!

Thank you for participating in this project. If it fails, that’s okay. You tried something, and that matters more than you could imagine. Thank you very much. Regardless of what happens, never stop trying!!!!
